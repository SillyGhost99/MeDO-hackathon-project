# MeDO-hackathon-project

✦ Neptune
Wear smarter. Style deeper. Look like yourself.
Neptune is an AI-powered fashion super-app designed to help you take full control of your personal style — from managing your wardrobe to discovering new looks, all in one place.

What is Neptune?? 🤔
✧Neptune brings together AI styling, social discovery, and a peer-to-peer marketplace into a single premium mobile experience. Whether you're building outfits from what you already own, exploring new aesthetics, or buying and selling clothing — Neptune makes it effortless, intelligent, and personal.
Features

✧Digital Wardrobe — Catalogue your clothes with AI-powered auto-tagging
✧AI Outfit Generator — Get outfit ideas built from your wardrobe or a style vibe
✧Reverse Clothes Search — Find and shop any item you spot in the real world
✧Personal Color Analysis — Discover the colors that suit you best
✧Outfit Calendar — Plan, log, and track every look you wear
Marketplace — Buy and sell clothing directly with other users
3D Virtual Try-On — See how outfits look on your body before wearing them
Social Feed — Share your looks, explore others, and get inspired
Built for people who take their style seriously.
